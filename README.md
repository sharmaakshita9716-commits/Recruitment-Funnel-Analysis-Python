Project overview:
This project provides a data-driven evaluation of a corporate recruitment funnel using Python. By analyzing a dataset of 1,500 candidates, the study identifies key drivers behind successful hiring decisions,audits the financial ROI of dfferent sourcing strategies, and verifies the fairness of the selection process.

Key Project Objectives:
Pipeline Diagnostics: Calculated a 31% Selection Ratio, revealing a highly efficient candidate filtering process.
Quality & Merit Audit: Confirmed that hiring is strictly performance-based by correlating Skill Scores and Interview Scores with final outcomes.
Talent Profiling: Determined that the organization prioritizes seniority , with median hire experience of 8.5 years.
Financial ROI: Analyzed budget allocation , identifying the 'Aggressive' strategy as the primary driver for 72.6% of successful hires. 
Diversity & Inclusion(DEI): Conducted a bias check with verified Gender Neutrality in hiring (30.9% Female vs 31.1% Male).
